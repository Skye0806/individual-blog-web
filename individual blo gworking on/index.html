By: SheLeads Team






We are a group of previous program managers and facilitators. After experiencing the difficulty of assessing program impact, we developed SheLeads. We provide particpants a safe and engaging space to share experiences. We provide organizations program impact metrics to maximize participant development and reach funding goals.
